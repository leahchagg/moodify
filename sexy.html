NAO

NAO is a British singer, songwriter, and producer. She was born on November 29, 1987, in East London, United Kingdom. Nao's music is a blend of electronic, R&B, and funk, with soulful vocals and catchy melodies. She first gained attention with her debut EP "So Good" in 2014, and since then has released two full-length albums, "For All We Know" in 2016 and "Saturn" in 2018. Nao has been praised for her unique style and powerful live performances, and has collaborated with artists such as Disclosure, Mura Masa, and Little Simz.
